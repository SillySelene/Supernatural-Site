Wizardry is a comical psuedonym given to a class of thought surrounding the Supernatural as a science that humans dont have a understanding over yet. there are several groups of wizardry, which can be accessed as seperate pages on the navbar above. note that, while psychobiology and psychoaugmentation are closely related fields, they use different techniques and have seperate focuses. psychoaugmentation is focused on combat skill and strength with narrow scope in other things, while psychobiology has a wider focus on altered biological and psychological states, which gives a wide range of possibility.
